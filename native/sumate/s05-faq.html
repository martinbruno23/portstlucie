Before you join

The honest questions.

Is the deposit really refundable?
Yes. The founding deposit is held, not spent. Ask for it back any time before the first match in 2027 and you get a full refund — no questions, no fees.
What happens in 2027 when the club debuts?
Your founder status and number carry over for life. Founders with a deposit get priority on season tickets and charter pricing locked in — you were here first, and it stays that way.
Do I have to live in Port St. Lucie?
No. Anyone can be a Founder, anywhere. Most founders are on the Treasure Coast, but the wall has names from Miami and beyond. If you believe in the project, you belong.
When do I get my founder number?
The moment you join — free or with a deposit. Your number, name and digital card are generated right away, and your name appears on the wall in real time.
What’s the difference between joining free and leaving a deposit?
Free makes you a Founder today — number, wall, digital card. A deposit also reserves your seat for the 2027 debut with priority access. Same founder status; the deposit just holds your place in the stands.
